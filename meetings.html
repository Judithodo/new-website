    LAS VEGAS MEETING SPACE & CONFERENCE ROOMS

    MONUMENTAL MOMENTS

    Impeccable views and sophisticated surroundings are the standout characteristics of each venue within our hotel. The perfect setting for your next meeting or event, we provide several event space options to suit every type of gathering, offering intimate penthouse meeting rooms, lavish boardrooms, functional banquet halls, Las Vegas conference rooms, and outdoor terraces. One of the most innovative Las Vegas business hotels, we have the flexibility to help you host an inspired business meeting in the Trump Boardroom or a glamorous cocktail reception in either of our Empire & Hudson penthouses. Our team of meeting professionals will support you every step of the way, leaving no detail overlooked, as you successfully aim to impress your attendees.

    Each of our Las Vegas meeting spaces are outfitted with state-of-the-art technology to seamlessly meet your every need and our culinary team can design a catered menu to perfectly suit the style and mood of your banquet or function.

    Shipping Services

    Our Guest Services team is here to coordinate your meeting and event executive shipping services. To begin the process, kindly complete and submit a Shipping Form, and our Bell Desk will promptly assist you.

    Meeting & Event Planning

    Even the most well-planned event can be better with the watchful eye and specialized attention of Trump Las Vegas. Using all the resources at our disposal, and our proven expertise at planning events both large and small, our professional staff will craft your occasion into the affair you've always imagined and never will forget at one of the most exclusive five star hotels in las vegas.
